Research

My research explores how investor behaviour, asset markets, and luxury consumption patterns reflect broader macroeconomic dynamics. I am particularly interested in how wealth is managed, signaled, and preserved across economic cycles, and how high-end assets like art, fashion, and alternative investments serve not only as status symbols but also as economic indicators.

I focus on understanding the financial strategies of affluent individuals and institutions, especially during periods of uncertainty. Shifts in portfolio allocation, luxury demand, and asset preferences offer valuable insights into investor sentiment and the underlying conditions of the global economy.
